I'm a french ph.D student in computer vision and deep learning at the University of technology of Troyes and the start-up Aquilae. I'm currently working in the domain of industrial unsupervised anomaly detection especially for textures.

My projects

Industrial textile dataset

This project introduce a new dataset dessigned for fabric anomaly detection. It contains 10 categories of textile with a wide variety of defects extracted from a textile factory with high resolution cameras.
